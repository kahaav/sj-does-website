## Services

### 1. Strategy
*Clarity first.*
Step back, see the big picture and build a strategy that actually moves the needle. No fluff — just clarity, direction and results.
**Tags:** Clarity · Direction · Growth

### 2. Marketing
*Smart. Human.*
Thoughtful marketing that connects, converts and builds brand authority — without the noise. Predominantly LinkedIn-first, with a whole-firm lens.
**Tags:** Brand · Positioning · Campaigns

### 3. Social Media
*Strategic. Intentional.*
A single source of truth across platforms to unify your brand. LinkedInterventions that build visibility, spark conversations and create impact.
**Tags:** LinkedIn · Content · Engagement

### 4. Legal Tech + AI
*Smarter choices.*
Survey → interviews → findings report → market research → recommendations. Helping law firms and in-house teams cut through the noise and implement what works.
**Tags:** Discovery · Evaluation · Adoption

### 5. Recruitment
*The right fit.*
Connecting exceptional legal talent at partner level with the right opportunities. Mostly lateral partner moves — where relationship and judgement matter most.
**Tags:** Partner Search · Lateral · Match

### 6. Coaching
*Confidence. Growth.*
Interview coaching for legal professionals at all levels using a 5-stage framework. Show up, stand out and own the room — from NQ to Partner. Delivered via phone or video call. Sessions start at £100/hour.
**Tags:** Interview Prep · Framework · Mindset

## About Suzanne

Suzanne Jeffers is a qualified solicitor with over a decade of experience in and around the legal sector. After qualifying during the pandemic, she made the decision to step away from traditional practice — not away from law, but towards something more useful to it.

SJ DOES is the ecosystem she built. Strategy, marketing, legal tech, recruitment and coaching — all connected, all rooted in a deep understanding of how law firms actually work and what they actually need.

She's been in the room. She knows the pressures. And she brings real commercial awareness — the kind earned through experience, not borrowed from a textbook.
